## Branding Guidelines

### Logo Concepts
1. **Phoenix Theme**: Rising bird, fire, transformation
2. **Path Theme**: Winding road, compass, journey markers
3. **Technology Theme**: Neural networks, circuits, digital elements
4. **Nature Theme**: Trees, mountains, sunrise, growth
5. **Unity Theme**: Connected circles, hands, community symbols

### Color Schemes
1. **Calming Blues and Greens**: Trust, healing, nature
2. **Energizing Orange and Yellow**: Hope, energy, positivity
3. **Professional Blue and Gray**: Trust, reliability, stability
4. **Warm Purple and Pink**: Creativity, compassion, healing
5. **Earth Tones**: Grounding, stability, natural healing

### Typography Recommendations
- **Primary**: Clean, modern sans-serif (accessibility)
- **Secondary**: Warm, friendly rounded fonts
- **Avoid**: Harsh, cold, or overly decorative fonts

## Platform-Specific Naming

### Mobile App Names
- Keep under 30 characters for app stores
- Consider searchability and ASO (App Store Optimization)
- Avoid special characters or numbers

### Web Application Names
- Should work well as a URL
- Easy to remember and spell
- Professional for healthcare settings

### Internal/Staff Portal Names
- **[AppName] Professional**
- **[AppName] Dashboard**
- **[AppName] Admin**
- **[AppName] Clinical**

## Trademark and Legal Considerations

### Name Verification Checklist
- [ ] Check trademark databases (USPTO, international)
- [ ] Verify domain availability (.com, .org, .app)
- [ ] Check social media handle availability
- [ ] Google search for existing businesses with similar names
- [ ] Consider healthcare industry naming conventions
- [ ] Ensure name doesn't conflict with medical terminology

### International Considerations
- Verify name meaning in other languages
- Check for negative connotations globally
- Consider cultural sensitivity
- Plan for international expansion

## Final Recommendations

### Top 3 Overall Recommendations

1. **ClearPath** (Primary Choice)
   - Easy to remember and spell
   - Positive, forward-looking meaning
   - Works well with "ClearPathRecovery.com"
   - Professional yet approachable
   - Good for both client and staff portals

2. **Phoenix Recovery** (Secondary Choice)
   - Strong symbolism of rebirth and transformation
   - Well-known metaphor for recovery
   - Inspiring and hopeful
   - Good branding potential

3. **MindBridge** (Alternative Choice)
   - Connects technology and mental health
   - Suggests bridging gaps in recovery
   - Modern, tech-forward feel
   - Unique in the healthcare space

### Naming Strategy
- Start with primary recommendation
- Conduct user testing with target demographic
- Consider feedback from healthcare professionals
- Finalize based on legal clearance and domain availability
- Plan for potential rebranding if needed
